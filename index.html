Business: Registering an LLC or corporation varies by state. The IRS governs federal taxes, while states may have additional rules. Key laws include the Internal Revenue Code and the Sarbanes-Oxley Act for compliance.

Job: Most foreign workers require visas like H-1B or O-1. U.S. labor law includes minimum wage laws (FLSA), anti-discrimination laws (Title VII), and OSHA safety standards.

ğŸ‡®ğŸ‡³ India

Business: Requires GST registration, business PAN, and Udyam for MSMEs. Popular forms include sole proprietorship, partnership, and private limited company. Key laws include the Companies Act 2013 and Goods and Services Tax Act.

Job: Labour laws differ for each industry. Employees benefit from minimum wages under the Minimum Wages Act, Provident Fund under EPF Act, and Employee State Insurance (ESI) Act.

Living: Affordable lifestyle, mix of private and public services. Aadhar and PAN cards are key documents. The Citizenship Act governs citizenship and naturalization.

ğŸ‡©ğŸ‡ª Germany

Business: Requires registration at the trade office (Gewerbeamt), and often a tax number. Excellent for startups, especially in tech. Key regulations include the Handelsgesetzbuch (HGB) and GmbH law.

Job: The EU Blue Card allows non-EU professionals to work. Strict labor laws (Arbeitsrecht) ensure employee rights, paid vacation, and healthcare.

Living: High standard of living, public health insurance, and strong public infrastructure regulated by Sozialgesetzbuch (SGB).

Business: Requires local office address and Articles of Incorporation. Foreign investors often need a business manager visa. The Companies Act and Commercial Code apply.

Job: Visas vary by profession. Japan has strict work culture, high expectations, and strong respect for seniority. Labor Standards Act governs employment.

Living: Safe and efficient. National health insurance covers most medical expenses. The Health Insurance Act regulates health care.

Business: Easy federal or provincial registration. Start-Up Visa supports innovative entrepreneurs. Key legislation includes the Canada Business Corporations Act.

Job: Express Entry for skilled workers. Employment standards include minimum wage, safety rules, and EI benefits regulated by the Canada Labour Code.

Living: Multicultural, with universal healthcare and quality education under the Canada Health Act.

Living: Modern cities and tech, but censorship and social scoring systems may affect life. Social Security Law regulates welfare.
